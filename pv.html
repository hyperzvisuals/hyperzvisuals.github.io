Privacy Policy

Effective date: August 7, 2025

1. Quick summary

This site (hyperzvisuals.com) respects your privacy. We collect minimal data needed to run the site, respond to inquiries, and improve our work. We do not sell your personal data. If you want the short version: browse safely — we only keep what's necessary.

2. Data we collect

  • Contact info — name, email, message when you use the contact form.
  • Form metadata — timestamp, IP address, user-agent (used to prevent spam and abuse).
  • Analytics — anonymous usage data (page views, referrer, basic device info) collected by analytics tools if enabled.
  • Cookies — small files to maintain sessions, preferences, and anonymous analytics.
  • Optional — if you explicitly upload files or share additional details, we keep those for the purpose you provided.

3. How we use your data

  • To reply to messages you send through the contact form (support, quotes, project inquiries).
  • To protect the site from spam, bots, or abuse.
  • To analyze and improve site performance and user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations if required (court orders, law enforcement).

4. Cookies & tracking

We use minimal cookies to keep the site functional. This may include:

  • Session cookies — to keep form states and UI consistent.
  • Analytics cookies — optional, anonymous (can be disabled).

If you use the site in private mode or block cookies, core pages still work — some convenience features may not persist.

5. Third-party services

We may use third-party providers for specific tasks (analytics, form processing, hosting). These providers have their own privacy policies. Typical providers include hosting/CDN, form processors, and optional analytics services. We choose vendors who follow standard security practices.

6. Form handling & storage

Contact form submissions are sent to a mail endpoint or form service. We retain those submissions for a limited time (typically up to 24 months) to support any follow-up or billing needs. You can request deletion at any time — see "Your rights" below.

7. Your rights

You have the right to:

  • Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data.
  • Object to processing or ask to limit processing where applicable.

To exercise these rights contact: hyperzvisuals.contact@gmail.com. We'll respond within a reasonable time (typically 30 days).

8. Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect data (HTTPS, server hardening, limited access). No system is 100% secure — if a breach occurs we will notify affected parties and authorities where legally required.

9. Data retention

We store personal data only as long as needed for the purposes described (inquiries, legal requirements, dispute resolution). If you request deletion, we will remove your data from active systems and retain only what is legally required.

10. Minors

The site is not directed to children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors. If you believe we have, contact us and we will delete it.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Effective date" at the top will change when we do. Substantive changes will be highlighted on the website or communicated directly when possible.
